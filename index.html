CiaoyuToggle.js

Please visit https://github.com/Canboo/CiaoyuToggle.

單選 radiobox

result:

A Zone
data-ciaoyu-toggle="example1"
or
data-ciaoyu-toggle="example1,example3"
B Zone
data-ciaoyu-toggle="example2"
or
data-ciaoyu-toggle="example2,example3"

複選 checkbox

result:

A Zone
data-ciaoyu-toggle="checkbox1"
B Zone
data-ciaoyu-toggle="checkbox2"
C Zone
data-ciaoyu-toggle="checkbox1+checkbox3"
D Zone
data-ciaoyu-toggle="checkbox1,checkbox2"

下拉 select

result:

A Zone
data-ciaoyu-toggle="ExSelect" data-ciaoyu-val="1"
B Zone
data-ciaoyu-toggle="ExSelect" data-ciaoyu-val="2"
C Zone
data-ciaoyu-toggle="ExSelect" data-ciaoyu-val="2,3"

result: (不是特定數值就出現請使用 ! 符號)

A Zone
data-ciaoyu-toggle="ExSelect" data-ciaoyu-val="!0"

混用 checkbox + select

請勾選 Agree 後選擇 選項1

result:

Agree and Select 1
data-ciaoyu-toggle="mixCheckbox+mixSelect" data-ciaoyu-val="1"
