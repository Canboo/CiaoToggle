CiaoToggle.js

Please visit https://github.com/Canboo/CiaoToggle.

單選 radiobox

result:

A Zone
data-ciao-toggle="example1"
or
data-ciao-toggle="example1,example3"
B Zone
data-ciao-toggle="example2"
or
data-ciao-toggle="example2,example3"

複選 checkbox

result:

A Zone
data-ciao-toggle="checkbox1"
B Zone
data-ciao-toggle="checkbox2"
C Zone
data-ciao-toggle="checkbox1+checkbox3"
D Zone
data-ciao-toggle="checkbox1,checkbox2"

下拉 select

result:

A Zone
data-ciao-toggle="ExSelect" data-ciao-val="1"
B Zone
data-ciao-toggle="ExSelect" data-ciao-val="2"
C Zone
data-ciao-toggle="ExSelect" data-ciao-val="2,3"

result: (不是特定數值就出現請使用 ! 符號)

A Zone
data-ciao-toggle="ExSelect" data-ciao-val="!0"
